The Avar language uses a Cyrillic-based alphabet. Below are various Latin transliteration schemes used for writing Avar in Latin script.

Cyrillic avar.me Scientific Typing 1932 Umarilov Turk Said Google Dict Wiki

Notes

  • The avar.me variant is used on this website
  • The Scientific variant follows academic transliteration standards
  • The Typing variant uses only ASCII characters for easy input
  • The 1932 variant is the historical Latin alphabet used 1928-1938
  • The Google variant is used by Google Translate (— = not observed)
